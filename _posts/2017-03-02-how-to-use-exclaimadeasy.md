This blog post explains how to use ExClaiMadEasy to submit your claim expenses.
* Go to the homepage.  
![Glimpse]({{ site.url }}/assets/home-page.png)  
* Sign in or Sign up using your email-address. You can also login using Google credentials.
* Choose ``Expense Category``. Weekly allows a maximum of 10 entries (*means, for a week!*). Choose accordingly.
![Glimpse]({{ site.url }}/assets/Application-page.png)
* Fill in the required fields (*mandatory fields are indicated on UI itself*).
* Click ``Next`` button.
* Now select the date for your bill entries. For example, you have a cab bill for 27th of Feb, select that date & then enter the amount for morning & evening. Enter ``000.00`` if you didn't board any cab.
![Glimpse]({{ site.url }}/assets/add-entry.png)
* Click ``Next`` button. It will show you that an entry for that date has been added.
![Glimpse]({{ site.url }}/assets/colored-buttons.png)
* If you feel you have incorrectly entered details, press Edit/See Records button & make changes there. After confirming the change(s), go back.
![Glimpse]({{ site.url }}/assets/edit-records.png)
* Keep on adding entries for the bill dates you have (*Remember to choose correct ``Expense Category``*).
* When you are done adding all the entries, press ``Generate Bill`` button to download the PDF file (this file contains the claim submission date in its name).
* You are done :-) Take a print-out of the form, sign & submit :-)
