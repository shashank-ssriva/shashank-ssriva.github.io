--- layout: default title: lognotify --- {% include analytics.html %} lognotify is a simple utility (& my first ever Python script) that displays a pop-up notification showing me the exact error message in real-time while I am working on my PHP web-app. This utility is very simple, small & highly customisable. You can customise it to tailor to your specific needs. For example, you can customise it to monitor your system logs, Apache logs, MySQL logs & potentially any log-file.
